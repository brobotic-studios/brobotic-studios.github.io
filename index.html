Klobbe!

Klobbe! is an upcoming 2D endless runner game for mobile devices. You play as Klobbe, a funny looking tank with big dreams of going up into the sky. Klobbe! has a delightful storybook art style and fluid gesture controls, and he can't wait to meet you!
