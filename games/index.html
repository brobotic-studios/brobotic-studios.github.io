Klobbe!

Klobbe! is a 2D endless runner games. You play as Klobbe, a funny looking tank with big dreams of going up into the sky. Klobbe! has a delightful storybook art style and he can't wait to meet you!

Tiny Journey

A microgame made in 24 hours. Based on a far more popular game, Tiny Journey will take mere minutes and is completely played in-browser and in 2D.

Dig, Danpe! Dig!

An upcoming grave digging arcade game. You play as Danpe, the gravedigger, and it's your job to bury the many many bodies that have gathered up on The Pile. Illustrated with fantastic paper-craft graphics and written with a delightfully macabre sense of humor
